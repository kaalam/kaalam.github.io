Detailed Description

A block. Anything in Jazz is a block. A block is a JazzBlockHeader, followed by a tensor, then in case cell_type == CELL_TYPE_JAZZ_STRING followed by a JazzStringBuffer, and followed by a JazzAttributesMap.
